# Global Semiconductor & AI Infrastructure Intelligence

## Overview

The global economy is entering a new era of **AI Sovereignty** and **Techno-Nationalism**, where semiconductor supply chains, geopolitical tensions, energy infrastructure, and AI investments increasingly determine national competitiveness.

This project develops an integrated **Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Framework** designed to analyze strategic vulnerabilities across the global semiconductor ecosystem between 2015 and 2025.

By combining trade flows, sanctions, national AI investments, energy capacity, macroeconomic indicators, and corporate exposure data, this repository provides a comprehensive view of the risks and opportunities shaping the future of the AI economy.

---

## Business Problem

Governments, technology companies, investors, and policymakers face several critical questions:

* Which countries are becoming bottlenecks in the global semiconductor supply chain?
* How do sanctions and export controls affect international trade flows?
* Which AI investment strategies are exposed to infrastructure and energy constraints?
* Which countries are likely to emerge as future AI leaders?
* How can organizations improve supply chain resilience in an increasingly fragmented geopolitical environment?

This project aims to answer these questions through a combination of risk intelligence, business analytics, forecasting, and decision-support modeling.

## Dataset Overview

The project integrates six interconnected datasets covering the period 2015–2025.

| Dataset                            | Description                                             |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Semiconductor Trade Flows          | Global semiconductor import/export activities           |
| Trade Sanctions & Export Controls  | Historical sanctions and technology restrictions        |

## Analytical Framework

The repository is organized into nine interconnected analytical studies.

### 01. Global Semiconductor Supply Chain Risk Intelligence

Develops a comprehensive risk intelligence framework that combines geopolitical, infrastructure, and supply chain indicators.

Key Outputs:

* Composite Risk Score
* Country Risk Ranking
* Supplier Risk Ranking
* Trade Route Vulnerability Analysis
* Executive Risk Dashboard

---

### 02. Supply Chain Dependency Analysis

Measures supplier concentration and dependency risks within the semiconductor ecosystem.

Key Outputs:

* Supplier Dependency Index
* Trade Concentration Metrics
* Critical Supplier Identification

---

### 03. Sanction Impact Analysis

Evaluates the impact of export controls and sanctions on international semiconductor trade.

Key Outputs:

* Trade Impact Assessment
* Sanction Exposure Analysis
* Policy Risk Monitoring

---

### 04. AI Infrastructure Readiness Assessment

Assesses national readiness to support large-scale AI development.

Key Outputs:

* AI Readiness Score
* Infrastructure Capacity Assessment
* National Competitiveness Benchmarking

---

### 05. Energy Bottleneck Detection

Identifies potential energy constraints affecting future AI expansion.

Key Outputs:

* Energy Stress Analysis
* Grid Constraint Assessment
* AI Energy Risk Ranking

---

### 06. Supply Chain Disruption Prediction

Applies machine learning models to predict high-risk semiconductor trade activities.

Key Outputs:

* Risk Classification Models
* Early Warning Indicators
* Explainable AI Analysis (SHAP)

---

### 07. AI Leadership Forecasting

Forecasts future AI competitiveness and national AI leadership trajectories.

Key Outputs:

* AI Sovereignty Forecasts
* Investment Growth Projections
* Future AI Leadership Ranking

---

### 08. Strategic Investment Recommendation

Supports investment prioritization through multi-criteria decision analysis.

Key Outputs:

* Investment Attractiveness Score
* Country Opportunity Ranking
* Strategic Allocation Framework

---

### 09. Geopolitical Scenario Simulation

Simulates alternative geopolitical and trade policy scenarios.

Key Outputs:

* Scenario Analysis
* Monte Carlo Simulations
* Strategic Risk Assessments
